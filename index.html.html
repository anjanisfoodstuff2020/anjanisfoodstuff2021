Coconut Suji Modak
नारळ रवा मोदक

Coconut Rava Modak is a very easy and simple modak mithai recipe which can be made during Ganesh Chaturthi as prasad. You need few ingredients for this recipe and it is even simple if you use a modak mould. This recipe is made from semolina and wet coconut and shaped in to either modak or laddu. Whichever form you choose they are simply delicious sweet. Do make it this festival season and pray for blessing for your family and loved ones to Lord Ganesh.
